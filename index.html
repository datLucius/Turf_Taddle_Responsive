    You play golf - we get blackmail

    PLAY GOLF, SERVE JUSTICE !

    • Tony Soprano
    • on
    • 4 19 1998

    Let's be honest. You love golf. You love money. Why not have both? I know what you're thinking - 'how can I have money, if golf costs money?' The answer - Turf & Taddle. When you join our team at T&T, you get an all access pass to the most luxurious private golf courses around the world. You'll be slappin' fives with Tiger, pounding down chamapgne with the super rich, gorging yourself on mountains of shrimp at the club house - you even get a caddy obedient to your every whim. It's the spot amongst the 1% you've always deserved!

    C.R.E.A.M.!

    'But I can't afford any of that!' you say? That's poor-man's speak. When you join the T&T team, we outfit you with everything you need to live the good life - an unlimited credit line, a backstory on your illustrious upbringing, fly threads, the works! Our goal is to make you happy. We only ask one thing in return. One small thing! Blackmail. All the blackmail you can gather from your wealthy comrades. They'll think you're someone special. Someone super wealthy and super awesome. You gain their trust on the green and later you return to us with some juicy tid-bits. It's that simple. 'Isn't that morally wrong?' Of course not!

    • MONEY MONEY MON-A

      Doubting that Turf & Taddle is right for you? Just take a moment and think about all the things you can do with money. Sure, there are the basics - paying bills, buying food, paying off loans. That's just the beginning! Turf & Taddle allows you to think bigger. Think what you could buy if money was no object. I'm talking jetskis. Hundreds of jetskis. You could buy an island and outfit a whole squad of private mail men with jetskis. You could pay them in lobster. Think about it!
